Improving onboarding flow

Case study — Research · UX · Prototyping

Problem

New users struggled to reach their first success within the product. Drop-off during initial setup reduced activation rates and impacted retention.

Approach

  1. Conducted contextual interviews with 15 users.
  2. Mapped the activation funnel and identified key friction points.
  3. Prototyped a simplified onboarding and ran a small A/B test.

Outcome

The redesigned flow improved time-to-first-value by 32% and increased activation by 12% in the test cohort.
